## **Signal and Channel**

* The Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR) compares the level of a desired signal to the level of background noise | Engineering | 0.95  
* Data Compression is the process of encoding information using fewer bits than the original representation | Processing | 0.94  
* Lossless compression allows the original data to be perfectly reconstructed | Compression | 0.96  
* Lossy compression reduces bits by identifying and discarding less important information | Compression | 0.95
